{{% callout note %}} 
At **ImpactFest 2025**, Reprex joined the expert session *“Brace for Impact: Trustworthy AI for Good.”*  
The discussion explored how **knowledge graphs** and **open, interoperable data** can empower AI and data-driven applications that serve both public good and private innovation.  
This continues our long-term collaboration with ImpactFest, where Reprex won the [Audience Prize in 2022](https://reprex.nl/post/2022-11-15-reprex-hague-innovators-award/).
{{% /callout %}}

### Key Idea
Data is only as powerful as it is **trustworthy**. Reprex promotes the use of **knowledge graphs** to connect open and proprietary data in transparent, reproducible ways that benefit both business and society.

### Next Steps
- Develop responsible AI applications built on shared, standards-based data.  
- Expand partnerships with European innovators in open and trustworthy data ecosystems.
